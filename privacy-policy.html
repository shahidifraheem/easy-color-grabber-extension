Easy Color Grabber - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-08-07

1. Information We Collect

Easy Color Grabber does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data about its users. The extension operates entirely locally within your browser.

2. Color Data

The extension may store recently picked colors in your browser's local storage for the purpose of maintaining a color history. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

3. Permissions

Easy Color Grabber requires the following browser permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current webpage content for color picking functionality
  • scripting: To inject the color picker script when needed

These permissions are used solely for the extension's core functionality and are not used to collect any personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

Easy Color Grabber does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The extension has no server-side components.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
